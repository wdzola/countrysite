ZOLA INFINITY

ZOLA Electric's Infinity system is the first modular, scalable solar and battery storage product that can power both heavy-duty AC and DC appliances, interconnect different energy generation sources, and create “clean” energy from intermittent power sources. After five years of engineering R&D, ZOLA’s newest system will be the first product in the African residential market that interconnects off-grid solar, micro grids, generators, and the macro grid

LEARN MORE TECH SPECS
  1. AC and DC compatible - The Infinity system is the first modular, scalable solar and battery storage product that can power both heavy-duty AC and DC appliances. Infinity uses proprietary micro-inverter technology to convert DC power, the current produced by solar PV, to AC power, which is required by mainstream appliances such as computers, refrigerators, and microwave ovens
  2. Interconnect energy sources - Infinity interconnects different energy generation sources and creates “clean” energy from intermittent power sources. Infinity can also regulate and control electrical power sources, including diesel generators and unreliable grid sources, to prevent surges and blackouts from voltage fluctuations and ensure a clean, reliable power source.
  3. Solar + Storage - The Infinity system stores solar power in high-density lithium ion batteries and uses advanced software to utilize the most efficient energy source at any one time. It regulates energy demand across generation, storage, and distribution.
